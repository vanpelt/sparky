Sparky

A collection of small tools and interactive projects, built mostly with the help of LLMs.

Interactive Visualizations

  • The Electromagnetic Spectrum
    Interactive guide to the full EM spectrum — from radio waves to gamma rays — with facts, practical uses, space connections, and an inverse-square law visualization.

Presentations

  • HiveMind — Engineering Overview
    Slide deck on HiveMind, an internal tool for capturing and sharing how engineers use coding agents.
